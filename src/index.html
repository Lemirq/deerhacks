HOW IT WORKS

Three steps from setup to signal.

üì∑

Capture

Your webcam grabs a frame every 2 seconds. The Grove Sound Sensor samples your voice level in real time ‚Äî no microphone array required.

‚Üí
ü§ñ

Analyze

Gemini 1.5 Flash Vision scores your energy, pacing, and eye contact from the frame + audio data. No cloud storage. No recording.

‚Üí
üí°

Signal

LED strip + buzzer fire instantly. No phone. No earbuds. No interrupting your flow ‚Äî just light and sound that your body learns to respond to.

HARDWARE

Built on a $120 Pi setup you can assemble in an afternoon.

Raspberry Pi 4 ¬∑ Main compute ‚Äî runs the full pipeline locally
USB Webcam ¬∑ Captures frames every 2s for Gemini Vision
Sound Sensor ¬∑ Grove analog sensor ‚Äî voice amplitude proxy
Light Sensor ¬∑ Detects ambient lighting for context
Grove Green LED ¬∑ GOOD signal ‚Äî positive reinforcement flash
Grove Red LED ¬∑ All warning signals ‚Äî solid or flashing
Grove Buzzer ¬∑ Tactile buzz patterns for each signal type
Grove Base HAT ¬∑ Plug-and-play sensor expansion for the Pi
